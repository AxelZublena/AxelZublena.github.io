How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

To print a document from your laptop at HZ you need: to know your MyHZ credentials and to have an HZ-pass with credit on it.

First connect to print.hz.nl and log-in using your credentials. Then upload a document supported by the platform (Microsoft Office, OpenOffice, PDF, JPG, GIF, PNG, TIF, BMP, TXT) or paste the link of the webpage you would like to print by following this link.

Follow the instruction and when you see the message “Your print job has been sent to the selected printer successfully.”, go to the nearest printer at HZ.
Finally, put your card on the stand close to the printer to authenticate yourself and print!

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ you need: to know your MyHZ credentials and to have an HZ-pass with credit on it.

Go to the nearest printer at HZ. Then put your HZ-pass on the stand close to the printer, and select scan on the screen. Follow the instructions. You will receive an email with your scanned document attached to it.

What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

If you show sign of sicknessm stay home and call in sick. If the symptoms get worse, contact your doctor. Also send an email to the international office if you are an international student (internationaloffice@hz.nl).You can still follow courses online.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

To book a project space at HZ you need to know your MyHZ credentials.

First, connect to MyHZ by following this link. Once you are connected, go to Services (see the menu at the top of the page) and then click on Helpdesk / IT facilities. You have now access to multiple categories. Click on “New reservation” followed by “Book project group rooms”. You now need to select a room, a date and to follow the instructions.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

To park your car at the HZ parking lot you need your HZ-pass. With you card, you can drive at, Poelendaelesingel 10 (300m from Het Groene Woud). After passing the barrier, follow the signs to the HZ parking lots. The HZ parking spaces have a white sign with the HZ logo. There are also a number of spaces available for recharging an electric car.

For more information see this link or contact Martine van Ommen.

How do bank cards work in the Netherlands?

When coming to the Netherlands you might want to use you credit or debit card. Unfortunately, chances are that you will not be able to use it. In fact, in the Netherlands, Visa and Mastercard bank cards are not compatible with the Maestro system. The reason for that is the following: Visa and Mastercard work on a dual-message system, while Maestro work on a single-message system.

There are 3 solutions to this problem. You can use you luck at the checkout (and risking an embarrassing moment), withdraw money at an ATM before buying something or create a Dutch bank account and therefore use a Maestro card.
