About Me

My name is Axel Zublena, I’m 18 years old and I have a twin brother. I’m French with a Guadeloupean root. I followed the standard French education path and specialized in science. I got the opportunity to choose between biology and engineering, and I went for engineering because biology is only French literature about rocks and frogs. During middle school I discovered computer science and explored every field of it. After high school, because I wasn’t in love with math and I didn't wanted to calculate the forces applied on a bridge all my life, so I went in the Netherlands to study software engineering at HZ University of Applied Science.

Automobile Racing

I knew for a long time that I like cars, but because my family isn’t really in the motorsport world and because I like computer, I never followed this path. So aside from computer science, I love every kind of motorsports (F1, GT, NASCAR, drag racing, drift...). Furthermore, there is now ultra-realistic racing video games which can be played with a steering wheel and a set of pedals. So, these last 2 years, I played at rFactor 2 which is a game used by professionals to train themselves. Like some before me, I was hoping to go from playing on my computer to driving in real life. One of my biggest achievement is participating in an official race organized by BMW Motorsport. But for now, I have set this passion in standby to focus on my studies at HZ.
