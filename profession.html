Microsoft

Microsoft is an American company with a rich history. Like every tech giant, Microsoft while based in the US is present all over the world and is composed of a wide variety of jobs and people. Microsoft use and create technologies.
CTO (Chief Technology Officer) and System administrator (Sysadmin) are two examples of jobs you can find at Microsoft.
Common technologies used by Microsoft are Artificial intelligence, Cloud and Operating system.

Google

Like Microsoft, Google is an american company and one of the biggest companies in the world. Google create and use all sort of technologies. The company is relatively young compared to other tech companies like IBM. Because the company is present all over the world, the american company is almost an international one.
Software developer and Data Scientist are two examples of jobs you can find at Microsoft.
Common technologies used by Google are Artificial intelligence, Cloud and web in general.

Orange

Orange is a French company. It was and still is a telecomunication company, but it now does all sort of things like internet banking and TV services. It's the biggest telecomunication company in France and is also implanted in other regions of the world like Africa.
System administrator (Sysadmin) and Software developer are two examples of jobs you can find at Microsoft.
Common technologies used by Orange are 4G and Optical fiber.

AMD

AMD (acronyme of Advanced Micro Devices) is, like every big company of this list, American. Main concurrent of Intel, AMD is an hardware manufacturer. Because AMD is a company focused on hardware, the main jb at this company is electrical engineer and not sofware developer like Microsoft or Google.
Electrical engineer and CTO (Chief Technology Officer) are two examples of jobs you can find at Microsoft.
The main technology used by AMD is the transistor.

Red Hat

Red Hat is an American company who create solution for linux and enterprise. The main difference between Red Hat compared to Google or Microsoft, is its philosophy. In fact, Red Hat is an open source company. This mean that everybody can see and sometimes edit the code made by Red Hat (this is the opposite of closed source/proprietary code made by most of the big tech companies.).
System administrator (Sysadmin) and Software developer are two examples of jobs you can find at Microsoft.
Common technologies used by Red Hat are Cloud and Operating system.

Macaw is a Dutch company that create solutions for enterprise. For me, Macaw is one of these companies where you don't really know what they do. It often looks like a lot of talking and not a lot of ground breaking technologies. But after I learned more about the company, I realized that Macaw (like others) is just focused on companies and not on you and me.
While Macaw is a Dutch company, it has an international philosophy. So while it's not the greatest company from my point of view, it's the one I prefer.

Veracode is a company focused on software security. Veracode is based in Burlington, Massachusetts in the USA and is not a small company with almost 750 employees. Technologies used by Veracode are the .NET framework and almost everything related to software developement. The biggest particularity of Veracode is the fact that almost every employee work remotely. While I really like cybersecurity, because working at Veracode mean working "alone" in my house, the company is not one where I would like to work at.

Your Surprise is a mix between traditional manufacturing and an IT company. Your Suprise needs a website to sell products the company manufacture itself. Your Surprise uses machine learning to predict its growth, a language they created (YIMP: Your Image Personalization) and web technologies. Like Macaw, Your Surprise attaches importance to the quality of life of its employees. This company looks fun to work for, but as an international student who wants to travel, Your Surprise is not for me.
